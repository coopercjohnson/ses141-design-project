Windmills turn kinetic energy of wind into rotational energy, which powers the generator that converts that energy to electricity
Plants turn chemical energy into mechanical energy through photosynthesis
Food turns chemical energy into mechanical energy that allows humans to move their muscles
Batteries turn chemical energy into electric energy
Turns electric energy into heat and light energy
Solar Panels are used to turn solar energy into thermal energy
Water turns gravitational potential energy (evaporation) into kinetic energy, which turns the turbine that activates the generator, converting it to electrical energy.
The sun converts chemical energy into solar energy by burning hydrogen into helium
